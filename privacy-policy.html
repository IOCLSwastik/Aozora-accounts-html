Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aozora Accounts. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile and desktop applications.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with a high-quality account management experience. This includes:

  • Account Information: If you use our cloud sync features, we store your account names and details.
  • Authentication: We use Firebase Authentication to secure your access. We do not see your passwords.
  • Usage Data: Basic information about how you interact with the app to help us fix bugs and improve performance.

3. How We Use Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Sync your account details across your devices.
  • Provide customer support when requested.
  • Improve the application features and security.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Google Firebase (Firestore) for database management and Google Cloud for infrastructure. We prioritize the security of your data above all else.

5. Third-Party Services

Aozora Accounts utilizes Google Firebase for core functionality. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at contact@aozoradesu.com.
