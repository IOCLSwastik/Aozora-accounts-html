Data Deletion Request

In compliance with Apple App Store and Google Play Store regulations, we provide users with the ability to delete their accounts and all associated data.

How to delete your data:

  1. Email contact@aozoradesu.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the email address associated with your account.
  3. Once verified, we will delete your account and all associated cloud data within 7 business days.

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
